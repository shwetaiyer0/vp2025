Your Weekly
Pause for Purpose

What is a
study class?

Join our Vedanta study classes in Pune to gain practical insights from the works of Swami A. Parthasarathy. These interactive sessions foster meaningful dialogue and insight, bringing ancient philosophy into clear, practical focus for everyday living.

About our
Teachers

Our teachers are graduates of the 3-year residential course at the Vedanta Academy, trained under the guidance of Swami A. Parthasarathy. They bring depth, clarity and real-world relevance, making this ancient knowledge accessible and meaningful for the modern seeker.

Have any
Questions?

WHAT IS THE DURATION OF THE WEEKLY CLASS?

The duration of the class is one hour, except when the Bhagavad Gita is taught, where the class duration is one hour and 15 minutes.

WHAT IS THE AGE ALLOWANCE FOR CLASSES?

Youth classes are conducted for 13 to 16 year olds. Adult classes are for 17 years and above.

HOW DO I REGISTER FOR A CLASS?

There is no registration required. Please connect with the respective class coordinator to confirm your attendance.
