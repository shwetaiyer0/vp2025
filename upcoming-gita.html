What is a
study class?

The talk on 25th May will be followed by weekly sessions of one hour each at a fixed time and venue. The sessions shall be on the text The Fall of the Human Intellect by Swami A. Parthasarathy. Each session invites open discussion and deep reflection —making the philosophy of Vedanta accessible, relevant and useful in daily life.

Your guide to
Peace and Purpose

The Bhagavad Gita is a manual for life. It offers timeless wisdom to overcome life’s challenges and guides one towards peace, purpose and productivity. Ultimately leads one to Self-realisation — the goal of human life.

Have any
Questions?

IS THERE A FEE TO ATTEND?

No, admission is free. All are welcome.

CAN I BRING MY CHILD?

Vedanta Pune conducts sessions for youth, 13 - 18 years old. View the session details here. If you are interested to begin a youth class in your area, please contact us on +917030407840

DO I REQUIRE ANY PRIOR STUDY OR READING BEFORE JOINING A CLASS?

No. However once you begin attending the class, you shall be advised by the teacher regarding the portion of the text to read and prepare before the next session.

WHAT IF I CANNOT ATTEND EVERY CLASS?

While it is most beneficial to ensure weekly attendance, if you are unable to make a particular week's class, you can catch up by reading the portion missed.
