What is Vedanta?

Vedanta educates you about yourself, the world and how the two interact. Its knowledge is relevant - whether you are making major decisions, managing stress, building relationships, or trying to find meaning in life. Above all, Vedanta leads you to the ultimate goal of Self-Realisation.

Vedanta Academy

In 1988, Swami A. Parthasarathy founded the Vedanta Academy at Malavli, near Pune. It serves as a world resource for Vedanta knowledge through its residential courses for youth, professionals and businesspersons.
