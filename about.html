The Vedanta Academy, founded by Swami A. Parthasarathy in 1988, is a world resource for study, research and dissemination of Vedanta. It offers continual three-year full-time residential courses and youth camps for students as well as corporate seminars and retreats for professionals and businesspersons. The knowledge is imparted through a liberal approach that enables the development of the intellect and not merely providing intelligence on a subject.

You are welcome to visit the Academy. For further information and to coordinate a visit, please email visit@vedanta.info

Swami A. Parthasarathy

Swami A. Parthasarathy (Swamiji), is a distinguished philosopher and a world-renowned authority on Vedanta. With a diverse academic background in literature, science, and law, he left a successful shipping career to dedicate over six decades to the study and dissemination of Vedanta.

Translating complex philosophical concepts into practical techniques for daily living, his teachings have benefited audiences around the world. Swamiji has authored twelve books, including bestsellers like Vedanta Treatise: The Eternities.

Sunandaji

Sunandaji, daughter-disciple of Swami A. Parthasarathy, has spent over 4 decades studying and teaching Vedanta worldwide. Known for her clear and powerful talks, she speaks at leading forums and universities across continents.

In Pune, she conducts annual Bhagavad Gita lectures, making its timeless wisdom accessible and relevant to the modern seeker.
